About Huffle’s IT Services

Huffle’s IT Services LLC is a disabled veteran-owned IT Support company built on porch wisdom, southern clarity, and comfort-coded care. We don’t claim to be experts—we claim to care.

Founded by Christopher Lagasse, our mission is to help real people feel safe with their technology. Whether it’s setting up a firewall, understanding encryption, or building a backup plan, we offer honest support with no shortcuts and no scare tactics.

We’re certified in foundational IT security (A+ and Security+), with Network+ and CySA+ in progress. Every page, overlay, and walkthrough is designed to be emotionally clear and technically sound—so you know what’s happening and why.

This site is porch-coded with care. That means every decision—from layout to language—is made with comfort, inclusion, and transparency in mind.

If you’re looking for support that feels like it was built on the front porch, you’re in the right place.
