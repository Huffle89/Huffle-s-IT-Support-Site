About Me

BUILT ON BACKBONE. RUN WITH HEART.

Welcome to Huffle’s IT Services—where tech support meets porch wisdom. I’m Christopher Lagasse, a disabled veteran and founder of this comfort-coded support company. I don’t just fix machines—I restore peace of mind. Every workflow is modular, emotionally aware, and mapped to real-world needs.

Whether you’re wrangling a stubborn Wi-Fi signal, setting up a new device, or trying to get your printer to behave, I’m there with tools in hand and a calm voice on the line. My goal is simple: deliver honest, grounded tech support with the kind of loyalty you’d expect from a front porch hound.

  • ✯ Veteran-owned and operated — service is in my bones
  • ✯ Southern-rooted support — no jargon, just clarity
  • ✯ Wolf-coded loyalty — I stick with you 'til it’s fixed
  • ✯ Comfort-first approach — every fix mapped to emotional and sensory boundaries
  • ✯ Modular overlays — every system built with layered logic and least privilege

Your technology should feel like a trusted neighbor, not a mystery. That’s why every service I offer is backed by porch logic, practical care, and a promise: I’ll treat your tech like it’s mine.
