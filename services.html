Our Services

Every service at Huffle’s IT Services is built to be modular, comfort-coded, and grounded in real-world experience backed by industry certifications (CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, CySA+, and related stackable certifications). As a disabled, veteran-owned business, work is planned around both technical best practices and realistic physical limits, for both home users and small businesses.

  • Typical Home User Flat Rates (Approximate): System setup $120; gaming/high-performance optimization $180; virus/malware removal $120–$200; data cleanup $100; data transfer $100; system rebuild $150–$200; smart home setup $40–$100; backup/restore setup (Acronis) $120–$150; specialized services (gaming, streaming, home labs) $100–$150.
  • Typical Small Business Flat Rates (Approximate): Workstation setup $175–$225; basic website help/edits $60–$120; inventory system setup $120; security & protection $125+; workstation rebuild $175–$250; backup/recovery setup (Acronis) $150+; advanced/virtualized environments $150–$250; QR code setup $40.
  • Custom System Builds: Parts deposit: full cost of parts (non-refundable once ordered). Labor usually $200–$400 for home users and $275–$400+ for small businesses, depending on the build.
  • On-Site Visit Fees: Home users: $60 flat-rate (includes first 30 minutes). Small businesses: $85 flat-rate (includes first 30 minutes).
  • Monthly Services: Website upkeep: $50–$75/month for home/personal sites; $100–$150/month for small businesses. Small business advisory support: $100–$150/month.
