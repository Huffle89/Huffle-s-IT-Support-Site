Our Services

Every service at Huffle’s IT Services is built to be modular, comfort-coded, and grounded in real-world experience backed by industry certifications (CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, CySA+, and related stackable certifications). As a disabled, veteran-owned business, work is planned around both technical best practices and realistic physical limits, for both home users and small businesses.

How Pricing Works

  • All work is quoted after a short consultation so you only pay for what you actually need.
  • Quotes are based on the kind of work, how complex it is, and how long it is likely to take.
  • You will always see and approve your quote before any work begins. No surprise charges.
  • Discounts are available for verified U.S. Military (active or veteran) and verified first responders (police, fire, EMS). Identification is required for verification.

Comfort-Coded Core Support

  • Comfort-Coded IT Support
    Gentle, patient troubleshooting for home users and small businesses, especially neurodivergent clients, veterans, elders, and anyone who feels overwhelmed by tech.
    • Help with slow or misbehaving computers, printers, software, and “something’s wrong” issues.
    • Support is paced and explained in plain language, with room for questions.
    • Work is scoped during consultation, then quoted before we begin.
  • System Setup & Optimization
    Professional setup and tuning of new systems and workstations so they’re ready for real-world use.
    • Initial OS and driver setup, updates, security configuration, and basic optimization.
    • Available for both home systems and small business workstations.
    • Custom performance tuning for gaming and high-performance needs available.
    • Physical limits apply; assistance may be required for large or awkward cases.
  • Custom System Builds (High-Performance PCs)
    Full custom builds for gaming rigs, family PCs, workstations, and small business systems.
    • Built around your needs, use case, and budget discussed during consultation.
    • Parts deposit is the full cost of parts paid upfront so hardware can be ordered.
    • Non-refundable deposit policy: once parts are ordered, the deposit is non-refundable, even if the build is canceled.
    • If vendor returns are possible, any refund is reduced by shipping and restocking fees.
    • Labor for the build is quoted separately and paid at delivery after the system is assembled and tested.
  • Security Consultation
    A structured review of your home or small business environment to identify risks and strengthen defenses.
    • Includes network, device, and account review guided by Security+ and CySA+ practices.
    • You receive clear recommendations in plain language, focused on your actual usage.
    • Optional follow-up hardening and written plans for small businesses are available.
  • Data Cleanup & Restoration
    Cleaning, organizing, and recovering data where possible so your systems feel lighter and safer.
    • Removes digital clutter, junk files, and unnecessary software.
    • Helps organize important files and checks drive health.
    • File recovery is attempted where technically possible, within reasonable limits.
    • Ideal before or after a rebuild, or when moving to a new system.
